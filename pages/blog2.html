Red Hat Work Experience Day 3

Wednesday Blog:o

today i continued on writing up my blog. I came in early so that i could have time to figure out how to use the blog and plan what i wanted to write. the first activity was in the conference room where we listened to a talk about AI. i found this really insightful as it gave me more of an understanding of the artificial intelligence that i'm using everyday. after the talk we were put into groups for team building. We were given the task of making a roller coaster with a loop for a ping pong ball. The first step was planning and after that it was to execute our idea using straws,tape,paper and tin foil. I was incredibly unsure if whether our idea was going to work or not at first because or barriers seemed too unstable. The first attempt failed miserably as the ball would not go over the loop. I had almost given up but we then decided to redo our design by basing it off another group who did theirs successfully and making a few changes here and there. This time it worked and i was extremely relieved after all the failed attempts previous. By doing so, i developed my problem solving and communication skills. I leart that things may not always work out the first try so you have to keep trying making adjustments here and there.Right after lunch were given a talk about student life and what SETU is like. I would have never considered putting SETU as a university for my options but after that I think it changed my perspective in going to a univeristy locally. There were a few challenges that I faced today but overall it was a useful experience that is applicable to everyday life.
