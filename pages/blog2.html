Monday Blog

Today was my first day of the work experience at Red Hat. Firstly in the morning we were given an introduction about what the company is about and then we did an icebreaker activity.i enjoyed this part as it gave me the opportunity to get to know new people and make friends. In the morning before arriving I was actually quite nervous but within the first hour I felt comfortable with the place and people around me.After we got a tour of the office and we were brought to our desks. I played around with the laptop to get used to the layout and I downloaded vscode and made my own Github account.We had break afterwards which i enojyed the most as i got to play on the Switch and the pool table as well as talking and getting to know the other girls. The most memorable part of today was probably the canteen area because of all the chocolate and snacks there.Ghost.
